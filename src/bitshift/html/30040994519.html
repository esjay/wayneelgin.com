Google ChromeOS devices can now play Google Play Videos

As confusing as it was, someone who owned a Google Chromebook could never have played their Google Play videos for the last year and a half, but Netflix and Hulu worked just fine (that speaks to just how ubiquitous Netflix strives to be). So, it’s good to hear that Google is making strides in supporting their own platform with their latest ChromeOS update.

Now, if only Google can find a way to support their own Google TV platform with Google Play videos, I think that would just about cover everything.
