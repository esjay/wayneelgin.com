FILDI

It’s easy to start something, but it’s hard to persist. Blogs are the worst.

In the spirit of all first blog posts, I’m going to be unrealistically optimistic and fail to meet my own expectations. The difference, I keep telling myself, is that I’m not going to stop once I fall short.

Something about streaming media here… in this space… every MWF.

That’s unrealistic. And it will certainly be wrong a lot. But it will not be wrong sometimes and that will be better than nothing.

I’m only doing this because I have a lot of opinions about digital content (video games on Steam, books on Kindle, movies on Netflix, apps on everything) and I think about it almost daily. I might as well start writing about it, I think?

It’s really cliche to say the future is now, because you knew that we’d be buying ones and zeroes 15 years ago. But, what happens after the now-future-from-yesterday? I don’t know. So, let’s start shifting some bits around.

Oh, and first!
