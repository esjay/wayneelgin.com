Valve's Desire to Remove Steam's Greatest Limitation (which is Valve, BTW)

Valve’s Gabe Newell was discussing the future of gaming, as he is wont to do, and made some statements that might seem jarring to– well, anybody at EA, for sure. But the gist is that nobody really wants Valve to curate the Steam catalog. They want what they want to be available and that the market would make sure to get their stuff from whomever is willing to give them that.
